Easily send mobile visitors to your Bourbon mobile website by installing and configuring the Bourbon Mobile Redirect plugin for Wordpress.

== Description ==

[Bourbon](http://getbourbon.com/) makes it a breeze to create an amazing mobile website your customers will love. To sign up for a 30-day trial (no credit card required), visit [getbourbon.com](http://getbourbon.com/).

# Features:
*   **Maps & Directions** — Customers can quickly map your location and get directions from wherever they are.
*   **Tap to Call, Email, Tweet** — It’s easy for your customers to connect with you by phone, email, twitter, and facebook.
*   **Easy to Read Menus** — Restaurant or cafe? Clearly display your menus, formatted beautifully for mobile displays.
*   **Image Gallery** — Don’t just tell customers what makes you awesome, show them with image galleries.
*   **Flexible Content Pages** — Use free form content pages to create any page, or set of pages, with fancy text and images.

== Installation ==

1. Upload `bourbon.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Sign into your Bourbon account, select Publish from the top nav, then copy your account token.
1. In your Wordpress website, select Settings -> Bourbon Mobile Redirect from the left nav. Paste the Bourbon account token into the Bourbon Token field and click Save Changes.
1. When your Bourbon mobile website is ready, select the Enable Redirect checkbox, and click Save Changes.
